Dragon Project Revision And Reflection

I showed project to a family friend they said that they were impressed with what I was able to come up with on my initial draft. One thing that was pointed out to me was they felt the color was a little to dark. I took this into consideration and lightened the fonts on my pages to make everything easier to read. I think that I am going to switch out the background color with a image in my version 2.

Working on this project has been a mix of a the things that I have learned over this semester. The skills that I have learned in HTML and CSS have served me well in this website design. I have learned to structure my content in a way that is meaningful and useful. I feel that I have done well in all the assignments that have been given to me. I feel like I would make the same overall decisions given the things I know now. I plan to incorperate the feedback given to me by both my instructor and peers and create a greater version 2
